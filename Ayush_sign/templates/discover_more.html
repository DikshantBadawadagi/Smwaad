How does HANDSIGNIFY work?

HANDSIGNIFY translates sign language gestures into text or speech, bridging the communication gap and helping you learn in the process.

Yes! HANDSIGNIFY is designed to work seamlessly across all platforms, making sign language translation a breeze everywhere.
